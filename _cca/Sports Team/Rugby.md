<h3>Rugby</h3>
<p>Rugby CCA aspires to groom young rugby players to be good team players
with sound decision making skills. Through rigorous trainings and competitions,
our rugby players will gain experience and learn more about themselves
to become more resilient, disciplined and confident.</p>
