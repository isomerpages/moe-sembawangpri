<h3>ICT Club</h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/ict01.png">
</div>
<p>The ICT Club equips students with essential digital literacy and digital
media skills through engaging, hands-on learning experiences. Students
learn to use technology creatively, effectively, and responsibly, while
developing computational thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills.
They also explore the effective use of AI and digital tools to prepare
them for learning and working in an increasingly digital world.</p>
