<h3>Choir</h3>
<p>The Sembawang Primary School Choir is a vibrant and passionate community
where students develop confidence, teamwork and musical excellence through
singing and performance opportunities. Choir members are given the platform
to explore vocal techniques, harmony singing, stage presence and expressive
performance while building friendships and a strong sense of belonging.</p>
<p>Through regular rehearsals and meaningful performance experiences, students
learn discipline, resilience and collaboration as they work together towards
a common musical goal.</p>
<p>The choir also nurtures an appreciation for the performing arts and provides
opportunities for students to represent the school at both school-based
and national events. Through singing, students &nbsp;are encouraged to
discover their voices, grow in confidence and experience the joy of making
music together as one ensemble.</p>
