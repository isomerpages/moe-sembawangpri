<h3>Chinese Dance</h3>
<p>Chinese Dance CCA aspires to groom little dancers who are passionate about
not only dancing but also the movements specific to the Chinese dance and
culture. These budding dancers will have opportunities to perform their
talent in public.</p>

<p>Welcome to Chinese Dance CCA!</p>
<p>Step into the enchanting world of Chinese dance where tradition meets
creativity! Our CCA offers a dynamic blend of traditional and contemporary
styles, providing an opportunity to explore China's rich cultural heritage
through movement and expression. Join us for a journey of discovery, teamwork,
and artistic growth as we dance our way to new heights together!</p>
