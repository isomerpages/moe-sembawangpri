<p>Dear Parents</p>
<p>Photograph(s) or video image(s) of students, their parents/guardians and
students’ work may be captured during school activities and events such
as classroom lessons, CCA, school camps and school concerts. The school
may use and publish such photographs and/or video recordings in school
publications, website, social media channels, and other communication channels.</p>
<p>Thank you.</p>
