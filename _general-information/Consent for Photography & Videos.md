<p>Dear Parents</p>
<p>We would like to inform you that throughout the course of the year, photograph(s)
or video image(s) of you and your child may be captured at all school events.
We may use and publish such photographs and/or video recordings in school
publications, website, social media channels, and other official school
communication channels. For clarifications, please feel free to contact
the school.</p>
<p>Thank you.</p>
