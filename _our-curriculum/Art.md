<h3>Art Curriculum</h3>
<p>In SBPS, we believe that art is integral to the holistic development of
every student. Through art education, we nurture 21st century knowledge,
skills and dispositions, and we aim to enable every student to enjoy art,
communicate visually and make meaning through connecting with society and
culture.</p>
<p><strong>Civic, Global Awareness and Cross-Cultural Literacy</strong>
</p>
<p>Students will explore artworks from different cultures and histories,
deepening their awareness and appreciation of both local and global communities.
This helps them develop a sense of belonging and identity, while encouraging
them to find their own voice and imagination as they explore and create.</p>
<p><strong>Critical, Adaptive and Inventive Thinking</strong>
</p>
<p>Through art making and art discussion, students will develop their critical,
adaptive and inventive thinking. During art-making, they will experiment
with different materials and techniques to express their ideas, learning
to take creative risks and think flexibly when solving problems. In art
discussions, they will practise reasoning, considering different perspectives,
and reflecting critically on artworks — skills that are valuable in all
areas of learning.</p>
<p><strong>Communication, Collaboration and Information Skills</strong>
</p>
<p>Art has its own language, and students will learn to use it to interpret
and share their ideas. Through various artistic activities, they will also
develop communication and collaboration skills — learning to be open to
different perspectives, work respectfully with their classmates, and take
shared responsibility for their work.</p>
<p><strong>Our Programme Over 6 Years</strong>
</p>
<p>By the end of their 6 years of art education, all students will be able
to see, express and appreciate Art. They will learn from a rich variety
of artists, including Singapore and international masters, contemporary
artists, and even fellow primary school students, through three themes
that broaden in scope as they progress through the school:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Self and Immediate Environment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Singapore Past, Present and Future</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The World and Region We Live In</p>
</li>
</ul>
