<h4><strong><u>Developing Enhanced 21st Century Competencies</u></strong></h4>
<p>Anchored on strong character education, the school places emphasis on
the development of 21st Century Competencies (21CC) to prepare students
to thrive in a rapidly changing, digital and interconnected world.</p>
<p>These competencies enable students to develop the skills, knowledge and
dispositions required to thrive in future learning, work and life contexts.
The 21CC framework focuses on:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Critical, Adaptive and Inventive Thinking</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Communication, Collaboration and Information Skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Civic, Global and Cross-Cultural Literacy</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/21cc.png">
</div>
<p>As the school embarks on the implementation of 21CC, these competencies
are embedded across students’ whole-school experiences during curriculum
lessons, Co-Curricular Activities (CCAs), and cohort-level programmes.
Through a coherent and sustained approach, the school aims to nurture confident,
future-ready learners who are grounded in values and equipped to contribute
meaningfully to society.</p>
