<p><strong><u>Experiential Learning Week (ELW)</u></strong>
</p>
<p>The Experiential Learning Week (ELW) provides our students with engaging
and meaningful real-life learning experiences that extend learning beyond
the classroom. During this period, regular curriculum lessons are temporarily
suspended as students participate in a range of carefully curated programmes
and hands-on activities designed to ignite curiosity and deepen learning
through authentic experiences.</p>
<p>Anchored on the development of E21CC competencies, ELW aims to nurture
essential 21st century skills such as communication skills, collaboration
skills, critical thinking, adaptive thinking, inventive thinking and cross-cultural
literacy. Through meaningful tasks and immersive experiences, students
are given opportunities to apply what they have learnt in authentic contexts
while exploring different learning environments and approaches beyond textbooks
and the traditional classroom setting.</p>
<p>ELW also provides opportunities for students to reflect on their learning
and demonstrate our school values through their actions and interactions.
These enriching experiences make learning engaging and enjoyable, while
nurturing confident, resilient and future-ready learners.</p>
