<p><strong>Trips For Internationalisation Experience (TIE) Programme</strong>
</p>
<p>The school designs meaningful internationalisation experiences with a
focus on destinations across Asia — particularly ASEAN countries, China,
and India for our students, as part of our TIE programme. These experiences
are thoughtfully designed in alignment with the Ministry of Education's
Learn for Life movement and Future of Learning vision, aimed at equipping
students with the skills and perspectives needed to thrive in an interconnected
world. Through these immersive opportunities, students develop Emerging
21st Century Competencies (E21CC), particularly in building global awareness
and cross-cultural literacy.</p>

<p><strong>Objectives Of Tie Programme</strong>
</p>
<p>To help our students</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; develop&nbsp;<strong>curiosity</strong>&nbsp;for
the world around them and a deeper appreciation of the diverse cultures,
histories, and growing economic opportunities across ASEAN countries, China,
and India, reflecting the spirit of a&nbsp;<strong>Reflective Contributor</strong>.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; embrace a&nbsp;<strong>growth mindset</strong>&nbsp;through
exposure to new environments and perspectives, building the&nbsp;<strong>resilience and excellence</strong>&nbsp;needed
to navigate an interconnected world.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; strengthen their&nbsp;<strong>global and cross-cultural literacy</strong> and
gain a greater awareness of Singapore's place in the wider region and the
contemporary realities that shape our world today.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; cultivate a sense of&nbsp;<strong>care and responsibility</strong>&nbsp;that
extends beyond our shores, deepening their appreciation for their roots
and their commitment to contributing meaningfully to their communities
— values central to a&nbsp;<strong>Compassionate Leader</strong>.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; develop a stronger sense of&nbsp;<strong>gratitude, integrity, and purpose</strong>,
and be ready to lead themselves and others with confidence and compassion.</p>
<p>In Year 2026, our school organised two Trips for Internationalisation
Experience (TIE) — one to Guangdong, China, and another to Kuala Lumpur,
Malaysia. These immersive journeys offered our students the opportunity
to explore the rich cultures, histories, and innovations of our neighbouring
regions, deepening their global awareness and cross-cultural literacy as
they engaged meaningfully with the world beyond our shores.</p>
