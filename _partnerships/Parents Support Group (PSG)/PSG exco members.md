<p>Parents, being key stakeholders, are an important link between the school
and our children. In our journey to provide our children with a holistic
education, we believe in forging a strong working relationship with parents.</p>
<p>The partnership with parents aims to support parents so that are they
are able and are empowered to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>recognise and fulfill their responsibilities as parents and play an active
and valued role in their children’s education</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>make their views known about how their children are educated</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>have access to information, advice and support during decision-making
processes about their children’s education or special educational provision.</p>
</li>
</ul>
